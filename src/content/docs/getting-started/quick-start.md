# Quick Start Guide

This guide will help you get started with our services in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Review Our Services

Before booking a consultation, we recommend reviewing our available services:

| Service             | Duration  | Price        | Best For            |
| ------------------- | --------- | ------------ | ------------------- |
| 1-Hour Consultation | 60 min    | $150/hour    | Strategy & planning |
| Technical Support   | Flexible  | $100/hour    | Implementation help |
| Code Review         | 2-3 hours | $200/session | Project assessment  |

## Step 2: Prepare Your Questions

To make the most of your consultation, prepare:

- A clear description of your project or challenge
- Specific questions you'd like answered
- Any relevant code, documents, or context

## Step 3: Book Your Session

1. Navigate to our [Services page](/services/)
2. Choose the service that best fits your needs
3. Click the booking button to schedule your session
4. You'll receive a confirmation email with meeting details

## Step 4: Join Your Session

On the day of your consultation:

- Join the meeting 5 minutes early
- Have your materials ready to share
- Be prepared to discuss your goals and challenges

## What to Expect

During your session, you can expect:

- **Focused attention** - Our experts dedicate their full attention to your needs
- **Actionable advice** - Walk away with clear next steps
- **Follow-up resources** - Receive relevant documentation and resources
