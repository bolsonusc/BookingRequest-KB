# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and policies.

## Booking & Scheduling

### How do I book a consultation?

1. Visit our [Services page](/services/)
2. Choose the service that fits your needs
3. Click the booking button
4. Select an available time slot
5. Complete the booking form

### What if I need to reschedule?

You can reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the session. Contact us via email or use the reschedule link in your confirmation email.

### What's your cancellation policy?

- **48+ hours notice**: Full refund
- **24-48 hours notice**: 50% refund
- **Less than 24 hours**: No refund

### Do you offer weekend appointments?

Currently, we offer sessions Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST. For urgent needs, contact us to discuss weekend availability.

## Sessions & Delivery

### How are sessions conducted?

All sessions are conducted via video call using Zoom or Google Meet. You'll receive a meeting link in your confirmation email.

### What should I prepare before my session?

- A clear description of your project or challenge
- Specific questions you'd like answered
- Any relevant code, documents, or context
- A stable internet connection

### Will I receive a recording?

Session recordings are available upon request. Please let us know at the start of your session if you'd like it recorded.

### Do you provide written summaries?

Yes, for sessions over 1 hour, we provide a written summary of key points and action items within 24 hours.
